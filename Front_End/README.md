# NPC Canteen Frontend

A mobile-first static PWA prototype for the NPC Canteen system.

## Included dashboards

- Landing screen with Customer and Trader role selection
- Customer Home with search, categories, and product cards
- Customer Cart with quantity controls and mobile money payment options
- Customer My Orders with reference numbers and status badges
- Trader PIN Login
- Trader Overview with summary cards and recent orders
- Trader Alerts for new orders and payment actions
- Trader Transactions with search and status filters
- Trader Products for visibility toggles and price management
- Trader Export with date/status filters and preview summary

## Demo notes

- Open `index.html` in a browser.
- Demo trader PIN: `1234`
- Product visibility and price edits on the Trader Products screen affect the customer shop screen.
- New customer checkouts appear in Trader Alerts, Transactions, and My Orders.
